FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about Google Business Profile setup and management. Still confused? Just WhatsApp us!

What is Google Business Profile and why do I need it?
+
Google Business Profile (formerly Google My Business) is a free tool from Google that lets your business appear on Google Search and Maps. When someone searches for your business type in your city, a well-optimized GBP helps you show up in the local pack — the top 3 results on Maps. It's the #1 way local customers find businesses in India today.
How long does it take to set up my Google Business Profile?
+
We complete the full GBP setup within 48 hours of receiving your business information. The Google verification process can take an additional 3-7 days depending on the method — video verification is fastest (same day), while postcard verification takes 5-7 days.
My business is already on Google. Can you still help?
+
Absolutely! Many businesses have a basic GBP listing but it's incomplete or poorly optimized. We audit your current profile, identify gaps, and optimize it with the right keywords, categories, photos, and content so it starts generating more calls and visits from Google.
Will my business appear higher on Google Maps after optimization?
+
Yes — in most cases, our clients see a noticeable improvement in Google Maps rankings within 4-8 weeks of optimization. Results depend on your location, competition, and consistency of updates. Our Growth and Authority plans include ongoing work to continuously improve your local rankings.
How do you get more Google reviews for my business?
+
We create a simple review request strategy — a short WhatsApp message or SMS template you send to happy customers with a direct link to your Google review page. We also set up a QR code for your counter or visiting card. Everything is 100% ethical and follows Google's policies — no fake reviews, ever.
My GBP account is suspended. Can you help?
+
Yes, GBP suspension is one of our specialties. We first audit your profile to identify the reason for suspension (which is often a policy violation like an incorrect address, multiple listings, or keyword stuffing). Then we clean up the violations and submit a reinstatement appeal to Google on your behalf.
Is Google Business Profile really free? What do you charge for?
+
Yes — the Google Business Profile listing itself is 100% free from Google. What you pay us for is the expertise, time, and strategy to set it up correctly, optimize it to rank higher, and manage it consistently. Most business owners either don't have the time or don't know the best practices — that's where we add real value.
Do you work with businesses outside Agra?
+
Yes! We work with businesses all across India — Delhi, Mumbai, Bangalore, Lucknow, Jaipur, and hundreds of smaller cities and towns. Everything is managed remotely over WhatsApp, so your location doesn't matter at all.
