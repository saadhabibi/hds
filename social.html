FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our social media services. Still confused? Just WhatsApp us!

What information do I need to share with you?
+
Just send us your business name, logo (if you have one), brand colors, and a brief about what your business does. If you have specific offers or products to promote, share those too. We'll handle everything from there.
Do you manage our social media accounts too?
+
Our main service is creating and designing the posts, which we deliver to you via WhatsApp. If you want us to post directly on your account, we can do that too — just mention it when you enquire and we'll include it in the plan.
How many revisions can I request per post?
+
The Basic plan includes 2 revisions per post. The Growth plan includes unlimited revisions — we keep refining until you're fully satisfied with the design.
What formats and sizes do you deliver posts in?
+
We deliver posts in all standard social media sizes — Instagram Square (1:1), Portrait (4:5), Story (9:16), Facebook Post, and WhatsApp Status sizes. All delivered as high-quality PNG or MP4 files.
Do you create posts in Hindi or regional languages?
+
Yes! We create posts in Hindi and English, and can accommodate Hinglish content too. If you need posts in other regional languages, let us know and we'll see how we can help based on your requirements.
Can I request urgent or same-day posts?
+
Yes, we can do urgent posts for occasions, flash sales, or last-minute announcements. WhatsApp us with your requirement and timeline and we'll do our best to deliver as quickly as possible.
