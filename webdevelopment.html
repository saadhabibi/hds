Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our web development services. Still have doubts? Just WhatsApp us!

How much does a website cost?
+
Our pricing depends on the type and size of the website. A basic 1-page landing website starts from an affordable price. Multi-page websites and eCommerce stores have custom pricing based on requirements. WhatsApp us for a free, no-obligation quote tailored to your business.
How long does it take to build a website?
+
A 1-page landing website is typically ready within 24-48 hours. Multi-page business websites take 5-7 working days. eCommerce and custom websites take 10-15 days depending on features and content provided by the client.
Do you provide domain and hosting?
+
Yes! Our Growth plan includes 1 year of free hosting. We also assist with domain name registration and DNS setup. If you already have a domain or hosting, we can work with that too — no problem at all.
Will my website work on mobile phones?
+
Absolutely. Every website we build is 100% mobile-responsive. We test on multiple screen sizes including smartphones and tablets to ensure your website looks and works perfectly for all your visitors.
Can I update my website content myself?
+
Yes, if you choose a WordPress-based website, you'll be able to update text, images, and blog posts yourself without any coding knowledge. For HTML websites, simply send us the updated content on WhatsApp and we'll update it promptly.
Will my website show up on Google?
+
Yes — we set up basic on-page SEO for every website we build, including meta titles, descriptions, heading structure, and Google Search Console submission. For stronger Google rankings, our monthly SEO plans can help you climb higher over time.
Do you build websites for all types of businesses?
+
Yes! We have built websites for shops, clinics, coaching centers, restaurants, salons, real estate agents, law firms, manufacturing companies, and many more. Whatever your business type, we can design a website that fits your needs perfectly.
What happens after my website is launched?
+
After launch, we provide 30-60 days of free support depending on your plan. You can request content changes, small tweaks, or technical fixes during this period. After that, we offer affordable monthly maintenance and SEO plans to keep your site growing.
