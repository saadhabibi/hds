FAQ

Aksar Pooche Jane Wale Sawaal

Marketing ke baare mein common doubts clear karte hain. Aur bhi poochna ho toh WhatsApp karo!

Digital marketing mein kitna budget chahiye?
+
Minimum budget ₹5,000/month se shuru ho sakta hai. Ideally, Google/Facebook Ads ke liye ₹10,000–₹25,000/month ka ad spend plus hamaari management fee — yeh combination best results deta hai local businesses ke liye.
Kab tak results dikhne lagte hain?
+
Google aur Facebook Ads se pehle week mein hi leads aana shuru ho sakti hain. SEO typically 2-4 mahine mein results deta hai. Social media growth 1-2 mahine mein visible hoti hai. Hum realistic expectations set karte hain.
Kya aap ads ka budget bhi manage karte ho?
+
Haan! Hamaari management fee alag hoti hai, aur aapka ad budget directly Facebook/Google ko jaata hai — humare account mein nahi. Poori transparency ke saath hum aapke budget ko optimize karte hain taaki maximum ROI mile.
Kya monthly contract hota hai ya lock-in period?
+
Hamaare plans monthly hain — koi long-term lock-in nahi. Agar aap satisfied nahi hain toh 30 days notice dekar band kar sakte ho. Lekin hamare results dekhte hue clients typically 6-12 mahine se zyaada rehte hain.
Kya mere paas website hona zaroori hai?
+
Nahi, hum website ke bina bhi marketing kar sakte hain — Google Business Profile aur social media se leads generate ho sakti hain. Lekin ek website hone se conversions kaafi zyaada ho jaate hain. Zaroorat pade toh hum website bhi banate hain!
Kaunse businesses ke liye digital marketing best hai?
+
Doctors, clinics, restaurants, clothing shops, real estate, coaching centers, beauty salons, hardware stores, marriage halls — basically har local business ke liye digital marketing kaam karta hai.
